11/**
12 * @brief Uniform and time dependent active stress model.
13 *
14 * Defines an active tension that is constant in space but time dependent, i.e.
15 * @f[
16 * \Tact(t, \calcium, \fiberstretch, \fiberstretchrate, \astressstate) =

18 * @f]

35 };
